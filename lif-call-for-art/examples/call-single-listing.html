Submission Process:
Ann Korologos Gallery accepts electronic submissions only via email. Submissions are reviewed on a monthly basis. They do not accept artwork dropped off or delivered to the gallery. For more information regarding their submission process, visit their website.

About the Organizer:
For more than 20 years, the Ann Korologos Gallery has specialized in Western art and artists influenced by the West. Owner Ann Korologos, who bought the gallery in 2007 has created a warm and inviting space that has become a center of arts and culture in the Roaring Fork Valley…and beyond.
